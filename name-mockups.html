Naming explorations

Eight possible worlds for the sonic journey app.

Each direction keeps the core idea intact: intentional listening for altered states, emotional release, reflection, and integration.

Most direct: Sonodelic Most premium: Resonara Most app-like: Thrum Most luminous: Lumae

01 / Explicit category creator

Sonodelic

Sound that opens the state.

The clearest bridge between sound and psychedelic-adjacent transformation. It feels bold, memorable, and built to define a new category.

My read

Best directions to develop next

  1. Sonodelic Most category-defining. Best if you want the altered-state promise to be unmistakable.
  2. Audelic Most balanced. Direct, catchy, and less long than Sonodelic.
  3. Resonara Best if trust, practitioners, research, and emotional safety matter more than edge.
  4. Thrum Best pure app name. It needs a strong descriptor, but it sticks immediately.
