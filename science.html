The Science

Intentional listening, studied with care.

Acoustic States is informed by psychoacoustics, music neuroscience, neuroaesthetics, altered states, and nervous system regulation.

Psychoacoustics

We design with attention to rhythm, timbre, spatial depth, repetition, contrast, and expectation, because sound can shape attention and perception before language enters.

02

Music neuroscience

Musical structure can influence emotion, memory, imagery, and physiological arousal. Acoustic States uses those principles to build intentional journeys rather than passive playlists.

03

Altered states

Deep listening can support shifts in attention, embodiment, imagination, and self-reflection. The platform helps listeners prepare, listen, reflect, and integrate.

04

Research direction

We are developing studies to understand how immersive sound experiences affect emotion, embodiment, imagination, and wellbeing over time.

How we study listening

Designed around intention, response, and integration.

Each journey is shaped around a target state, but the listener's response matters most. Reflections help identify which sonic patterns support clarity, regulation, emotional release, creativity, or deep rest.

Acoustic States does not make clinical claims. It is a wellness and listening platform being built with scientific and ethical care.
